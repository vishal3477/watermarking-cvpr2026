Foundations and Frontiers of Watermarking

Algorithms, Multimodal Extensions, Benchmarks, and Authenticity Frameworks

CVPR 2026 Tutorial · Half-Day · In Person

Overview

Invisible watermarking has re-emerged as a core component of security tools in modern generative AI systems. This tutorial provides a unified end-to-end overview of watermarking, spanning classical signal-processing foundations, modern deep-learning and multimodal techniques, robustness evaluation and benchmarking, and real-world deployment in provenance frameworks such as C2PA and Content Credentials.

Our goal is to equip researchers and practitioners with a clear understanding of theoretical foundations, state-of-the-art methods, evaluation strategies, and practical deployment considerations for trustworthy media.
